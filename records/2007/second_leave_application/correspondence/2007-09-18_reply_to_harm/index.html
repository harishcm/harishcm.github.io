>> Some comments and stuff I'm unclear about though (maybe because I'm quite ignorant about the whole self directed learning method)

It's great that you brought up stuff that were unclear! Firstly, I don't think you are ignorant- there are no standard methods to self-directed learning. Everyone does it in his / her own way: self-directed.

As a whole, my methods are heavily influenced by the Unschooling and Homeschooling communitites. But I am definitely strongly influenced by other sources, particularly Schooling. So I guess, my methods will be kind of a mix of the above three. (Interesting that I look at them as '~methods~ of learning' here. haha!)

How did I come up with my methods? 
1. I got ideas from some source (books, friends, self & family & societal pressure)
2. I got the idea out of nowhere (those sudden sparks you get). I recorded it down on paper i carried around.. Then I organized these ideas while transferring them into electronic form. 
3. I tried to follow the methods, modifying what I do as the methods themselves evolved.
